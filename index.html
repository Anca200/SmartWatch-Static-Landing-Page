Frequently Asked Questions

What features does the smartwatch offer?

The smartwatch offers a range of features including fitness tracking, heart rate monitoring, GPS, notifications, music control, and more.

Is the smartwatch water-resistant?

Yes, our smartwatch is water-resistant up to 50 meters, making it perfect for swimming, showering, and everyday wear.

How long does the battery last?

The battery life of our smartwatch lasts up to 7 days on a single charge, depending on usage.

Can I customize the watch face?

Yes, you can choose from a variety of customizable watch faces to match your style and preferences.

Does the smartwatch support third-party apps?

Yes, our smartwatch supports a range of third-party apps available for download through the app store.

What is the warranty period for the smartwatch?

We offer a 1-year warranty on all our smartwatches, covering any manufacturing defects or issues.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package's status online.
